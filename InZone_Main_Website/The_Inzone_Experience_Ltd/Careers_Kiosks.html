Inzone’s Careers Kiosks

 

Premier Interactive Careers Display

Inzone’s high-tech Careers Kiosk provides New Zealand schools with up-to-date careers information and a fantastic multi-media view of a wide range of employment and training opportunities in New Zealand.


The state-of-the-art touch screen kiosks are easy to use and accessible to all year levels, providing guidance for school leavers who are undecided on their next step.

 

Younger students also have a unique opportunity to find a career they can be passionate about. Students can then select the correct subjects to maximise their potential to advance in these industries.


With Inzone finding a career is as easy as watching TV !
